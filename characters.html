Goku is introduced as a monkey tailed boy who practices martial arts and possesses superhuman strength.He ultimately meets a girl named Bulma as they try to find the seven Dragon Balls in order to accomplish getting a wish from the Dragon.
Vegeta is the prince of an warrior race known as the Saiyans. He is really arrogant and proud of his kind and hardworking. He will refer to his group of Saiyans frequently throughout the show.
Gohan is the oldest son of Goku. He is a hybrid child which is a Saiyan and Earthling. His name was chosen by Goku after his adopted grandfather.
Piccolo was the main antagonist to Goku in the Dragon Ball series. He is now a protagonist in the Dragon Ball Z and super series. He was responsible for training Gohan to help defeat Cell.
Krillin is a supporting protagonist in the Dragon Ball franchise. He is one of the most talented martial artists on Earth. He is faitful and loyal to his friends Goku, Gohan, and Piccolo. He is typically overpowered against major oppenents as you will find out in certain battles.
Cell was created by Dr.Gero with the ultimate goal of being the best fighter and conquering the Earth. He was made with a combination of the Greatest fighters that have ever fought. He was the main Antagonist during the Cell Games Saga.
Frieza is the emperor of the 7th Universe. He controlled his own army and is feared for his power as he is considered one of the strongest fighters in the universe. He has battled Goku multiple times in different Dragon Ball series.
